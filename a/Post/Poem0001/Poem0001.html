Poem0001

2023-03-10

Weird how inspiration comes I guess...

melancholy man of days gone by

how you dwell on the days till you die


winter draws near on a blistering day

but cozied by the strangers that come out to play


the dwelling that commence of temptation

and provides one of illicit irrigation

unites all in a homely vacation


what choices today

with what that we say


the regrets tomorrow

your destiny's sorrow


driven by desire, lust, or pain

worried of fate's permanent stain

realize myself, that i have nothing more to gain
