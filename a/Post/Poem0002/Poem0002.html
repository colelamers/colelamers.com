2023-03-21

Girl before a Mirror - Pablo Picasso Rot ist dein Herz und blau ist dein Blut, obwohl die wechselnden Farben das sind, was jeder will

My views stark, but returned with poise

Like the color green; not blue nor turquoise


Or as a mans rage has cooled from his head

What is turned pale or cream; whence came from red


How your wonder turns of curiosity for thine fellow

Of a tulips facade; eventual to that of a great yellow


Could the incessant feeling of being at a loss for fun

Remind you of an apples change; emerald to crimson


Those deceitful thoughts hidden beneath a verbal trade

Unfolds my invisible empire; not one of jade


One of the mind between a long beholden tryst

Is the jewel of my sentiment; a beloved amethyst
